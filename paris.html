Why I Love Paris

Paris is famous for its romantic atmosphere, rich history and beautiful architecture. Walking along the Seine and seeing the Eiffel Tower at night is an unforgettable experience.

The city is also full of art – from the Louvre Museum to small galleries hidden in quiet streets. Cafés on every corner make it easy to sit, relax and watch the city move around you.
